About Us

At Navan Frame Works, we believe every picture has a story worth telling. What started as a passion for art has grown into Salem’s trusted destination for premium custom framing solutions. We specialize in a wide range of services, including custom photo frames, intricately designed devotional frames, personalized gift items, and high-quality customized calendars. Using cutting-edge AI photo enhancement, we can restore and improve your old or blurry images, ensuring your memories look sharper and more vibrant than ever before. Beyond framing, we offer a curated collection of divine spiritual portraits and God statues, perfect for your prayer room or as a meaningful gift. With three branches across Salem, our mission is to provide professional craftsmanship that adds elegance to your home or office. Whether it’s a milestone celebration or a sacred deity for your home, we handle every piece with the care and precision it deserves.
